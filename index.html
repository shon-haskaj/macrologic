How quickly can we start?

Most audits can begin within a week. Small workflow builds usually launch in two to four weeks depending on tool access and approval complexity.

What tools do you work with?

We commonly connect CRMs, inboxes, forms, spreadsheets, finance tools, databases, internal dashboards, and custom APIs.

Do we need to replace our current software?

No. The goal is usually to make your current stack behave like one connected system before introducing new tools.

Who is this for?

MacroLogic is for teams with enough repeated operational work that manual copying, chasing, and reporting have become expensive.
